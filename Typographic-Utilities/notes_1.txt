watch out for rem and em of text in browser

- <h1 class = "h4"> behaves like it is h4 instead of behaving like h1
- display classes are used for showcasing 
    <h1 class="display-1"> text </h1>
    goes upto display 1 to disply 4
- <p class="lead"> paragraph containts </p>
    makes the paragraph prominent
- <p class= "text-monospace"> makes the paragraph of monospace characters </p>
- <p class="font-weight-bold"> Bold fonts </p>
- <p class="font-weight-normal"> normal fonts </p>
- <p class="font-italic"> italic fonts </p>
- <p class="text-lowercase"> MAKE LOWERCASE </p>
- <p class="text-uppercase"> MAKE UPPERCASE </p>
- <p class="text-capitaliZe"> prayag desai </p>
    makes the first letter of every word capital
-  ** blockquote which is right aligned
        blockquote class=" blockquote text-right"> This is a blockquote </blockquote>
    blockquote with footer and cite. used for proverbs and sayings and representing thoughts

        <blockquote class="blockquote text-right">
            <p>
                    "Satisfaction lies in the effort, not in the attainment, full effort is full victory."                   
            </p>

            <footer class="blockquote-footer"> Mahatma Ghandhi </footer>

        </blockquote>
